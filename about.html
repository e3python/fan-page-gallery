The Raw HTML Challenge

Exploring the fundamental building blocks of the web, one tag at a time.

Why Black & White?

You might notice that the student projects in this gallery look... simple. There are no colors, no fancy layouts, and no photos. This is by design.

Before an architect picks the paint color, they must pour the concrete foundation. In this unit, our engineers were challenged to build "Fan Pages" using strictly Semantic HTML. They were forbidden from using CSS styles to ensure they mastered the hierarchy of information first.
